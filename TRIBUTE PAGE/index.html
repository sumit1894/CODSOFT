Steve Jobs

1955 - 2011

About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and business magnate who was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. He was also the majority shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios and a member of The Walt Disney Company's board of directors.

Major Achievements

  • Apple Inc.: Co-founded in 1976, revolutionizing personal computing.
  • iPod: Launched in 2001, transforming the music industry.
  • iPhone: Introduced in 2007, redefining mobile communication.
  • Pixar: Played a crucial role in its success as a leading animation studio.

Legacy

Jobs' vision and innovation have left an indelible mark on technology and design. His relentless pursuit of excellence continues to inspire future generations of entrepreneurs and innovators.
